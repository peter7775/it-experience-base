### lsblk
`lsblk`

list disk filesystems
`lsblk -f`

### lshw (disk information)

`sudo lshw -class disk`
`sudo lshw -class disk | grep <disk_name> -A 5 -B`

### fdisk
`sudo fdisk -l`

### hwinfo

`sudo hwinfo --disk`

`sudo hwinfo --disk --short`
